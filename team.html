Dr. Dhananjay Kalbande

Dean - Industry Relations

Dr Dhananjay Ramrao Kalbande is currently a Professor and Head, Computer Engineering and Dean(Industry Relations), Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, Andheri (West), Mumbai, India. He has completed B.E. in Computer Technology from Nagpur University in 1997 and Master of Engineering in Information Technology in May 2005, from Vivekanand Education Societyâ€™s Institute of Technology (VESIT), Mumbai University, Mumbai, India. He has obtained PhD in Technology from The University of Mumbai, Mumbai in 2011. He has been awarded a Post-Doctorate from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences(T.I.S.S.) in 2016. He is also associated with T.I.S.S. as a Senior Research Fellow on the NCW-TISS The project, funded by the National Commission for Women, Govt. of India. He is having over 16+ Years of experience in teaching & research. His research interests include Soft Computing (Neural Networks, Fuzzy Logic), Computer Network, Human Machine Interaction Decision making and business Intelligence, Mobile application development for a social cause., ICT for semi-rural development for a social cause. He has authored three books namely Graphical User Interface(Pareen Publications), MIS(Pareen Publications), Human Machine Interaction(Wiley Publications), Digital Forensic(Wiley Publications) for Engineering Graduate students. He has delivered and conducted the workshop plus seminar talk on NS2, Neural Network,VB.Net and ADO.Net.
